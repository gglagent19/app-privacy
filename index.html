Privacy Policy — App Empire apps

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by App Empire / KerbLabs on the Google Play Store (the “Apps”): Last Done, Daily Chores, Moon Phase & Full Moon Alert, Repeat Checklist, Goal Timer, Safe to Spend, Bill Calendar, Want It, Run Out Radar, and Specs Keeper.

The short version

All of your data stays on your device. Our Apps do not have accounts, do not require sign-in, and do not send your personal content to us or to any server.

Information we collect

  • Data you enter in the App (chores, bills, prescriptions, items, time logs, etc.) is stored only locally on your device. We never receive it, see it, or transmit it. If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted.
  • We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or any personally identifying information.

Advertising (ad-supported Apps only)

Our free, ad-supported Apps use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as a device advertising identifier and general usage signals) to serve and measure ads. This is governed by Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites/apps that use its services

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads). Our paid / Pro Apps (Want It, Run Out Radar, Specs Keeper) contain no ads and use no advertising SDK.

Purchases

In-app purchases (Remove Ads / Unlock Pro) are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details — Google handles all payment information.

Children

Our Apps are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data security

Because your content never leaves your device, it is as secure as your device. We recommend using a device lock screen.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change accordingly.
