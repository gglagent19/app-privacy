Privacy Policy — Nazara (3D God Live Wallpaper)

Last updated: 6 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Nazara app ("we", "the app", package com.depthwalls.parallax) handles information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

The app does not require an account and does not ask you for personal information (no name, email, phone, contacts, photos, or location).

  • Wallpaper catalog: the app downloads publicly available wallpaper images and data from our cloud (Supabase) and caches them on your device. These requests are not tied to your identity.
  • Sharing: when you tap "Share", the app creates a short video on your device and hands it to the app you choose (e.g. WhatsApp). We do not upload or store your shares.

Advertising

This version of the app does not contain ads and does not collect an advertising ID or any device identifiers for advertising. If ads are ever added in a future update, this policy will be updated before that change and the store listing will reflect it.

Permissions

  • Internet — to download wallpapers from the cloud.
  • Bind wallpaper — required by Android to set and run the live wallpaper.

How information is used

Only to deliver wallpapers and run the live wallpaper. We do not sell your data.

Data sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. Cloud hosting is provided by Supabase.

Data retention & deletion

Downloaded wallpapers are cached locally and removed when you clear the app's cache or uninstall the app. We do not keep a personal profile of you. For any request, contact us below.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal data.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.
